Beautiful Beaches

Visit one of our beautiful beaches located only a short walk from the Tahiti resort.

Tahiti Valcano

Take a guided tour of Tahiti's local valcano and the surrounding area.

Fishing Tours

Rent a boat or take a guided fishing tour around the island. You are sure to have a great time.

Explore the Rainforest

Tahiti is home to a vast expanse of natural rainforests. Take a guided tour through the wilderness and learn about the island's history.

Great Local Pubs

Tahiti has several pubs and bars for individuals that want to enjoy a more traditional night life.

History Museums

Want to step back from the hustle and bustle and enjoy a quiet day learning about Tahiti and the islands history? Check out one of our many museums.
