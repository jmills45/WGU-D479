FAQs


You probably have a lot of questions about visiting Taniti. Thankfully, we've collected the most common ones here for you!


Still have questions? Feel free to contact us! We will do our best to answer any question you may have about a potential visit to the island.


Common Questions - Acohol


  • What is the legal drinking age?

    The legal age in Taniti is 18.

  • Do resturants server Alcohol?

    Yes, most dining establishments on the Island server some variation of alcohol.

  • Can I buy Alcohol on the Island?

    Yes, but you will not be able to purchasing from most stores between the hours of midnight and 9:00am.

Electronics and Conviences


Worried that your electronics won't work while on vacation? How about whether or not you'll be able to buy last minute necessaties?


  • Will my Electronics work in Taniti?

    Yes! Taniti uses 120volt power just like the United States

  • Will I need a power adapter?

    Nope! All your electronics should plug in and operate normally.

  • Are there stores available to necessaties?

    Yes! Taniti has several supermarkets and grocery stories, one of which is open 24hrs a day.

  • What if I get injured on the island?

    You don't need to worry. Taniti has its own hospital and several clinics.

Speak the Language?


You may be worried that you will have trouble communicating once you arrive on the island. There is no need to worry!


  • Will I be able to communicate with the locals / people

    Yes! Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, although Very little English is spoken in the more rural areas.

  • What about essential staff like at the hospital?

    All essentials surfaces have multiple, multilinqual employees on staff at all times.

  • What is the main currency on the Island?

    Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
