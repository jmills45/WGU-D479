Ground and Local Transportation


Worried that you won't be able to get around once you arrive to the island? Don't worry, Taniti has a large public transportation system at your disposal.


Transportation Information:


  • Call for Bus Schedules and Info

    Phone: 123-456-789

    Email: bus@taniti.com

  • Do the local buses require a bus pass?

    All local public transportation can be paid for via a public transport pass or with cash when arriving at your destination.

  • How do I get a bus pass?

    If you are staying at the Taniti resort, a bus pass will be provided to you when you arrive. Otherwise, a bus pass can be purchased at any bus station or goverment building.
