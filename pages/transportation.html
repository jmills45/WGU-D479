Ground and Local Transportation


Worried that you won't be able to get around once you arrive to the island? Don't worry, Tahiti has a large public transportation system at your disposal.


Transportation Information:


  • Call for Bus Schedules and Info

    Phone: 123-456-789

    All local public transportation can be paid for via a public transport pass or with cash when arriving at your destination.
