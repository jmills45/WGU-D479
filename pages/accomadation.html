Taniti Resort


Do you want an all inclusive vacation where everything is taken care of for you?


Stay at the Taniti resort where everything you may need is provided. Enjoy the beauitufl sights, amazing food, and all the luxaries you would expect out of a five star resort.


Resort Information:


  • Call or email now to book your reservation.

    Phone: 987-654-3210

    Email: resort@taniti.com

  • What is included at the resort?

    Everyone staying at the Taniti resort has everything they need to enjoy their vacation. Accomadations, food, and transportation are all included in the cost.

  • Is there a shuttle available from the Airport?

    Yes, an hourly shuttle travels back and forth between the resort and Taniti airport.

Taniti Hotels


If you are not interested in the Taniti resort, there are a wide range of Hotels available for you to stay at.


Hotel Information


  • Book your room today.

    Phone: 456-789-1230

    Email: hotel@taniti.com

  • What room sizes are available?

    Rooms are available in single king, double queen, and suite variations for up to 4 people per room.

  • Are meals included in the price?

    Meals are not included with your hotel stay, but a free breakfast is served between 6:00am and 11:00am.

  • When is checkout?

    All quests are to be checked out of their room by 12:00pm. If you need an extension please inform the frontdesk.

Taniti Hostels


Wanting to vacation in Taniti but don't want to break the bank? Consider staying at one of our many hostels. They are an comfortable and affordable way to visit the island.


Hostel Information:


  • Book your hostel today.

    Phone: 789-321-5460

    Email: hostel@taniti.com

  • Are private rooms available?

    Private rooms are available at all hostels, but they fill up fast.

  • Do hostels include a kitchen / shared facilities?

    Yes! You can expect all hostels to have a shared kitchen and bathroom area. Some also include a living area to relax in.

  • Are my possessions safe?

    All hostels are required to have a locked locker for your posessions. If you require more space for valuables, the hostel also has private rooms available.
